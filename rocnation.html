Why RocNation?

RocBoi Quez’s move to RocNation is more than a deal — it’s a declaration. As an independent powerhouse, Quez has cultivated a loyal following from Atlanta’s southside to global streams. Now with the backing of RocNation Distribution, he brings deeper licensing, bigger visibility, and a louder mic to the game.

The First Releases

The first wave of this collaboration includes three key records:

  • X-MEN – A lyrical banger that’s now set to go global.
  • All In – A motivational anthem about full commitment.
  • Get Out Your Feelings (ChpdNScrwd) – Paying homage to Houston while keeping it 100% ATL.

Strategic Vision

“This is about ownership, elevation, and legacy,” says Quez. “With RocNation, I stay indie but push major. We’re opening gates, not begging at them.”

Fans and new listeners alike can expect polished rollout campaigns, sync opportunities, and continued direct engagement through atlQuez.com.
