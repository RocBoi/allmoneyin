About the Track

"Summer of the Real" is a powerful summer anthem fusing authentic Atlanta street stories with a mainstream-ready sound. Featuring RocBoi Quez, Oreez, the iconic Wiz Khalifa, and breakout artists EaziDuzit and Lady NY — the song demands realness, celebration, and fire bars. A declaration of identity and resilience from the new voices in hip-hop.
