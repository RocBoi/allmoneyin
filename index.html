Slattland Records Insider: The Vision Behind "Stairs or Window"

RocBoi Quez delivers a cinematic reflection of his reality with the latest single “Stairs or Window”. The track captures raw emotion and choice-driven narratives in the streets of Atlanta. As described by SlattlandRecords.info, this release marks a pivotal moment in RocBoi Quez’s evolution from underground king to mainstream visionary.

From the minimalist production to the symbolic visuals — the stairs represent struggle, and the window signifies escape. "We all face that decision," says Quez. "This track makes you feel the weight of it."
