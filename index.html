About the Track

“Summer of the Real” isn’t just a song — it’s a statement. RocBoi Quez steps into the mainstream spotlight with a raw, unfiltered lyrical performance that proves why the streets have been whispering his name. Teaming up with none other than Wiz Khalifa — Taylor Gang’s own — along with breakout voices Oreez, EaziDuzit, and Lady NY, this anthem marks a defining moment for Atlanta’s next major voice. The track weaves melodic grit with heatwave bars that make it clear: RocBoi Quez is no rookie. He’s here. And hip-hop better take notice. “Summer of the Real” is the sound of arrival — bold, bossed up, and backed by Bentley Records IO.
