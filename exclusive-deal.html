RocBoi Quez Signs Exclusive Publishing Deal with DJ VIP

ATLANTA, GA — Rising hip-hop artist RocBoi Quez has officially entered a powerful new era in his career by signing an exclusive publishing deal with none other than DJ VIP — the legendary tour DJ and close collaborator of the late Nipsey Hussle.

DJ VIP (Anthony Orozco) is the driving force behind VipAccessPublishing, a publishing and music marketing powerhouse with strong ties to some of the biggest names in hip-hop. With proven success in label outreach, artist development, and campaign management, DJ VIP brings both legacy and innovation to the table.

Who is DJ VIP?

Known globally as Nipsey Hussle’s right-hand man and official DJ, DJ VIP has graced stages across the world while contributing to one of the most iconic independent movements in hip-hop history. His vision lives on through his publishing imprint, empowering new voices like RocBoi Quez to rise and claim their space in the game.

What This Means for RocBoi Quez

This partnership unlocks new doors for label partnerships, licensing deals, global music placement, and broader distribution. For RocBoi Quez, this is more than a contract — it’s a strategic elevation. Fans can expect greater exposure, more polished releases, and campaigns that match the energy of an artist ready to lead a movement.

Fan Anticipation is Real

The streets are buzzing. RocBoi Quez’s supporters — from Atlanta to LA and beyond — have been riding since day one. With this publishing deal now public, anticipation for his next drop has officially skyrocketed. The bar is higher. The movement is growing. And RocBoi Quez is positioned to lead.
